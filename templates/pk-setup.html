pk.fronters.cc

Want to make your own fronters.cc page that inferaces with your PluralKit system? It's easy! To start, you'll need to ensure that your system's fronters are PUBLIC. Go to the bot channel of any Discord server and enter the following command: 

pk;system privacy front public

Next, you'll want to find your PluralKit system ID. Enter the following command: 

pk;system id

Now you're all set! You should be able to find your fronting page at pk.fronters.cc/systemid. For an example, go to pk.fronters.cc/exmpl!
