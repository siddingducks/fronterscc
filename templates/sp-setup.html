sp.fronters.cc

Want to make your own fronters.cc page that inferaces with your Simply Plural account? To start, you'll need to generate a system token. Go to your Simply Plural dashboard on the web app. You will not be able to generate a token using the mobile app. Next, click on the Tokens button and click Add Token. Create a READ-ONLY token. Once finished, CLICK AND HOLD token to copy to clipboard.

Next, you'll need to find your user ID. Go to your Simply Plural dashboard and click on Account Settings. Scroll down to the section labelled Info. There, you will find your user ID.

Now paste your token and your user ID into the boxes below with NO SPACES. Choose your custom url, hit submit, and you're all set! You should be able to find your fronting page at sp.fronters.cc/customurl. Please note that this page will only display PUBLIC fronters.
