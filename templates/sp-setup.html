Setting Up with Simply Plural

Want to make your own fronters.cc page that inferaces with your Simply Plural account? To start, you'll need to generate a system token. Go to your Simply Plural dashboard in your web browser. You will NOT be able to generate a token using the mobile app. Next, click on the Tokens button.

Click Add Token.

Create a READ-ONLY token.

Once finished, CLICK AND HOLD token to copy to clipboard.

Next, you'll need to find your user ID. Go to your Simply Plural dashboard and click on Account Settings.

Scroll down to the section labelled Info. There, you will find your user ID.

Now paste your token and your user ID into the boxes below with NO SPACES. Choose your custom URL, hit submit, and you're all set! You should be able to find your fronting page at fronters.cc/sp/customurl. Please note that this page will only display PUBLIC fronters.

Want to change your token or your custom URL? Simply repeat the process detailed above with the new information and click submit. Your information will be updated upon submission. (Please note that you cannot change your user ID.)

Want to delete your fronting page? Enter the exact information you created the page with (user ID, token, and custom URL), and click the Delete My Data button. Your information will be erased upon confirmation.
